Hospital ER Dashboard - Power BI Project

This is the descriptive analysis of the Hospital ER dataset that shows the patient admission flow each year. It has KPI cards, Area charts slicers, donut chart, "heatmap" matrix, clustered bar chart and a line chart.
This is part of resource utilization of the staff schedulling dataset that shows the scheduling for staffs and how to better manage them while reducing workload and cost. It has KPI cards, clustered bar chart, a gauge, matrix and a scatter chart.
This is also part of resource utilization of the staff scheduling dataset. It shows the workload of the staffs and how many hours they are scheduled to work. It has clustered bar charts and a donut chart.
This is also part of resource utilization of the equipment usage dataset. It shows the usage of the equipment and the downtime of each equipment. It has a gauge, matrix, line chart, clustered column chart and a donut chart.
This is identifying bottlenecks of ER Wait time dataset. It shows the overall details on the wait time in the ER like the average wait time and average time spent in the ER. It has a matrix heatmap, scatter chart, key influnce visual, waterfall chart and clustered bar chart.
This is the predictive analysis of Hospital ER Data, staff schedulling and ER wait time dataset. It shows the overall prediction of the hospital ER, that includes ER wait time, staff scheduling and the patient admission flow into the hospital. It has a interactive slicer that syncs with the visual on the page, staffing recommendations, arrival forecasting and the wait time heatmap. It has slicers, matrixs and a line chart.
